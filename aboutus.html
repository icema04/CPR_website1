Know More About Us

CPR Heroes was founded with one powerful mission: to empower individuals to act in moments of crisis — whether it's a friend, a family member, or even a stranger.

We are passionate about making life-saving skills accessible to everyone. Through fun, engaging, and educational programs, we aim to equip our community with the confidence and knowledge to respond in emergencies.

Because every heartbeat matters — and anyone, anywhere, can be a hero.
