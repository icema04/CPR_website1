Fun Facts About CPR
  • CPR stands for Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation.
  • It was first developed in the 1960s and saves thousands of lives every year.
  • Immediate CPR can double or triple survival chances after cardiac arrest.
  • Hands-only CPR is a simple technique anyone can learn.
  • Learning CPR builds confidence and helps you react calmly in emergencies.
